Go-live step 3 (auth routes, dormant): POST /api/auth/login and /api/auth/logout, plus a signSession() cookie minter on the SessionPort seam. Login returns 503 ("not configured") unless XENTRAL_LIVE_DATA=1, DATABASE_URL and XENTRAL_SESSION_SECRET are all set; when armed it verifies email + bcrypt password against the existing "users" table and mints the HMAC-signed xentral_session cookie (httpOnly, secure, sameSite=lax, 7d). The /auth/login form now posts to it. Public preview keeps the flags unset → 503, no DB touch, seed only. After arming, cutover is just setting env + flipping nginx.
